About Us

Constellation presents live, interactive events around movies and other premium content online.

At the events, Constellation users can chat with each other, and interact with live hosts, such as filmmakers, actors, writers, and other taste-makers, in live Q+As after the movies. In some cases, the host also leads live commentary during the movie.

Studios, independent producers, and television companies have used Constellation to create exciting interactive events to promote and to sell their content.

Constellation was founded in 2011 by James Lawler and Reid Carolin and is based in New York City and Los Angeles.
