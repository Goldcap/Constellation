Vision

Community

Just like a traditional movie theater, audiences purchase tickets to attend scheduled showtimes of movies, or create their own showtimes to watch when they want. Unlike other online platforms, watching movies on Constellation is a social experience - users can invite their friends to watch with them, and can interact with VIP hosts, such as the films' directors or actors, who often appear live to answer questions during and after select showtimes.

Leverage Constellation

By uploading ancillary content – like photos and video clips related to your film, expand the likelihood of discovery, as people favorite your content on their profiles and interact with it on your page.

Market

We allow Constellation users to promote films, and earn money doing it by our affiliate system. Each film page has an affiliate link that is tied to the account of the user viewing your page. Every purchase of your film made through that link gets split 50/50 with your promoter. See a list of your top promoters on your film page.
