About Us

Constellation is the first global movie theater.

Just like a real theater, we show new films at specific times for live audiences. Anyone can buy a ticket, invite their friends, and chat with others in the theater at any time. Many films are hosted by filmmakers, stars, and other VIPs. In these screenings, the host leads a live video Q+A after the film where anyone from the audience can ask questions. You can also host your own screening and live video Q+A, and invite your friends.

For best results, make sure you are using the most up-to-date version of your browser software, and that you have Flash installed on your computer. You will also require a broadband internet connection.

Constellation was founded in 2011 by James Lawler and Reid Carolin.
