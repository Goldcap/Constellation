Flash files
This is where you will put the Limelight URL for your content (file).

  • The file should be an FLV
  • Use the "rtmp" protocol for Flash streaming
  • Use the "http" protocol for progressive download

SWF files
Insert the Limelight URL for the SWF that your downloaded from the LUX Flash Player page.

  • You will need to upload the SWF to your FTP account to be delivered by Limelight
  • You can deliver the SWF from your servers if you choose to do so
  • Limelight Flash players will only play files delivered by Limelight
